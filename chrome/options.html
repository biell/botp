OTP Secret

This software implements a single Time-Based One-Time Password algorithm. Secrets must be entered by code, this application does not have QR support. The secret is AES encrypted and stored in your browser's local stroage. Secrets are not synchronized across browsers. AES encryption is only as good as the password used to protect the secret, and should be considered a temporary protection measure. If your system is lost or stolen, you should report your OTP secret as potentially being compromised.
